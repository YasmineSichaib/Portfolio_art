About Me

Hi, I'm Yasmine. I'm 25 years old, and here I share with you all my paintings and my art.

Welcome to my world. My work is heavily inspired by manga culture, entirely shaped and hand-painted with acrylics on canvas. I believe in the magic of colors and putting my soul into every brushstroke.
